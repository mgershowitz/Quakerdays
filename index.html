QUAKE SKATE


Game play


THERE HAS BEEN A MASSIVE 10.01 EARTHQUAKE IN NEW YORK CITY. THE CITY IS FALLING APART, BUILDINGS CRUMBLING, HEROES ASSEMBLING. YOUR GOAL IS TO MAKE IT OUT ALIVE. WITH THE SPACEBAR JUMP OVER THE OBSTACLES AT THEIR INCREASING SPEED, WITH THE ARROW KEYS GO LEFT OR RIGHT. OVER COME ANYTHING OR ANYONE THAT GETS IN YOUR WAY IN ORDER TO MAKE IT OUT ALIVE!
